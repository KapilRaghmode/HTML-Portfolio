The Best Movies According to Kapil

My top 3 movies of all-time

Taare Zameen Par

It is a story of a dyslexic child and his struggles in life. It is a very emotional movie and I love it.

3 Idiots

It is a story of 3 friends and how they follow their passion in life. It is a very inspiring story and I love it

Sooryavansham

It is a story of a father and his son. It is a very emotional movie and I love it.
